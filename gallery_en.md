# Personal Visual Identity

## Theme

My personal brand combines several seemingly contradictory elements:

* Artistic
* Geeky
* Slightly Creepy
* Surreal
* Curious

These illustrations are not random decorations. Together they form a visual language that reflects how I think, learn, build systems, and explore the world.

---

# Visual Design Principles

## Color Palette

Purple serves as the dominant color throughout the series.

Supporting colors include:

* Black
* White
* Green
* Pink

Most illustrations intentionally limit themselves to five or fewer colors.

Transparency is the only color variation, while gradient fills are deliberately avoided. This creates stronger color blocks and sharper visual contrast.

## Hybrid Style

The illustrations combine two visual approaches:

* Hand-drawn manga-inspired line art
* Complex vector graphic structures

Objects with stronger emotional or character-driven elements are outlined using black comic-style lines, reflecting my long-term interest in anime and manga culture.

More abstract concepts are represented through geometric and vector-based forms.

## Contrast

The overall color strategy aims for maximum visual tension with minimal complexity.

Simple palettes are used to create strong contrast, memorable silhouettes, and immediate recognition.

# Butterfly of Expanding Vision

![Butterflies with eye-patterned wings spreading from a self-portrait, symbolizing curiosity and expanding perspectives.](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kotsuki-dev/kotsuki-dev/main/gallery/01-eye-butterfly-curiosity.jpeg?raw=true)

The central symbol of the entire brand.

A self-portrait grows a butterfly from the top of its head, representing thoughts extending beyond their original boundaries.

### Butterfly

The butterfly symbolizes:

* Curiosity
* Imagination
* Intellectual exploration

Its wings are composed of eyes of different sizes, representing the ability to observe the world from multiple perspectives.

### Eyes

The eye patterns continue expanding outward beyond the composition, symbolizing a continuously growing field of vision.

### Boundaries

The left and bottom borders are enclosed by black frames.

The top and right sides remain open.

This contrast represents structure coexisting with freedom, and boundaries that can still be crossed.

# Spiderweb of Thoughts and Connections

![A self-portrait woven into a spiderweb of thoughts, connections, and patterns.](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kotsuki-dev/kotsuki-dev/main/gallery/02-spiderweb-mind-connections.jpeg?raw=true)

A self-portrait covered by a giant spiderweb.

### Self-Portrait

The stiff, doll-like hairstyle, coupled with a world-weary expression, reveals a somewhat ironic self-image.

Comic-style linework hints at my affection for anime, manga, and geek culture.

### Spiderweb

The web symbolizes:

* Thought
* Connection
* Context
* Pattern recognition

It reflects a tendency to search for relationships and structures hidden inside complex systems.

It can also be interpreted as:

* Graphs
* Knowledge networks
* Neural connections
* Search systems

# Curious Butterflies in Another World

![A fragmented world map and butterfly, symbolizing global perspective and boundless imagination.](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kotsuki-dev/kotsuki-dev/main/gallery/03-otherworld-map-perspective.jpeg?raw=true)

A surreal world built from fragmented geography.

### Map

The world map is intentionally dismantled and rearranged.

It represents:

* Alternate realities
* Parallel worlds
* Virtual and physical spaces
* Different cultural perspectives

### Butterfly

The butterfly remains the core symbol.

Its eye patterns extend beyond one side of the image and reappear from another, suggesting exploration without boundaries.

### Taiwan

A small Easter egg is hidden within the map.

I come from Taiwan, but my perspective is not limited by geography.

# Roses and Ants

![Roses and hardworking ants, representing persistence behind flourishing results.](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kotsuki-dev/kotsuki-dev/main/gallery/04-roses-and-ants-craftsmanship.jpeg?raw=true)

Perhaps the least technological illustration in the collection.

It reflects a work ethic rather than a technical concept.

### Roses

Blooming roses symbolize visible achievements and meaningful outcomes.

### Ants

The ants represent contributors whose efforts are often overlooked.

They symbolize:

* Persistence
* Repetition
* Discipline
* Invisible labor

Large achievements are often built upon countless small contributions.

# Nepenthes and Dancing Fairies

![Dancing fairies atop a nepenthes, representing elegant challengers and small contributors.](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kotsuki-dev/kotsuki-dev/main/gallery/05-nepenthes-and-fairies-signal.jpeg?raw=true)

A metaphor for extracting signal from noise.

### Nepenthes

The carnivorous plant represents:

* Filtering
* Selection
* Information extraction
* Capturing valuable signals

Finding useful information inside overwhelming amounts of noise is a recurring theme in both engineering and learning.

### Fairies

The dancing fairies symbolize contributors who continue moving forward despite uncertainty and challenge.

Each ballerina represents a small but meaningful contributor whose efforts help create something larger than themselves.
